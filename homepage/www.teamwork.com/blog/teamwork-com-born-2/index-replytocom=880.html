Teamwork.com is Born!

Posted January 31, 2014

TeamworkPM was so named because of the underlying principles of how and why we designed the product – for collaboration and co-operation: the key components of a successful outcome to any project or operation.

So why a dot com?

For customer confidence we know how important a dot .com domain is. And Teamwork.com?

As happy as we are with TeamworkPM and as committed as we are to maintaining and driving features forward, we are not happy to rest on our laurels. We want to expand our product offering. We have big ideas!

We have learned a lot over the past seven years, and we will be applying that knowledge and experience to keep building on those very same principles. No other name would do, it had to be Teamwork.com. We already know from experience that customers refer to us as Teamwork…..now they are right!!

So we are proud to kick off the year with an almighty bang. On the 23rd of January we rebranded TeamworkPM.net to Teamwork.com, with a complete redesign of our website and the release of our Teamwork.com introductory video.

So what does this mean for you?

Well, the good news is that there are no major changes at all. You now just have the option to move over to the easier and nicer .teamwork.com domain for your account. Plus we also have SSL on this domain name as well.

Teamwork.com was born at 9:00pm on Thursday the 23rd of January. The party took place in one the most iconic Irish venues: The Guinness Storehouse.
